DexNote

Your all-in-one student hub.

DexNote brings together courses, notes, meetings, AI tools, and collaboration features in one unified platform built for students.

Courses

Learn through carefully guided and structured lessons designed to help you understand concepts deeply, practice effectively, and progress at your own pace.

Notes

Create, organize, and review your notes effortlessly, with tools to highlight, tag, and structure information for quick reference and long-term retention.

Meet

Join or host smooth and interactive study meetings with classmates, teachers, or mentors, complete with audio, video, and screen-sharing options.

Whiteboard

Collaborate in real time on a dynamic, shared whiteboard where you can write, draw, and brainstorm ideas with others seamlessly.

AI Helper

Get instant answers, step-by-step explanations, and personalized study guidance powered by AI to clarify doubts and strengthen your learning.

Tasks

Plan, track, and manage all your assignments, deadlines, and study goals efficiently to stay organized and productive every day.

Files

Store, organize, and access all your study materials, documents, and resources securely from one central location whenever you need them.

Chat

Communicate instantly with classmates, teachers, or study groups, share ideas, ask questions, and collaborate in real time to enhance learning.
